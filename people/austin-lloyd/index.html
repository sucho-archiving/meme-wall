Austin (Lloyd)

Lloyd Austin is the U.S. secretary of defense and a retired Army general (link to his Wikipedia page below). He is seen as a supporter of Ukraine’s cause in its defense against Russia’s full-scale invasion of the country, and he is sometimes invoked alongside his Ukrainian counterpart, Valerii Zaluzhnyi.
