Kiselev (Dmitrii)

Dmitrii Kiselev (also spelled Dmitry Kiselyov) is a Russian TV personality, the head of state-run media Rossiya Segodnya, and a propagandist (link to his Wikipedia page below). Memes about Kiselev will often invoke him alongside other supporters of Russian President Vladimir Putin, and some will point to eventual retribution for his role in supporting Russian aggression against Ukraine.
